Legalese

Legal

The bridge and oracles are operated by members of the Bismuth team.
The BIS on-chain data is the reference used.
The bridge is not custodial. Its function is to swap native BIS to ETH wBIS as well as ETH wBIS to BIS.
This is just a relocation from one chain to another and the change rate is 1:1 at all time, minus fixed operation fees.
It's not an exchange either. There is no order book, no market.
 
Due to its decentralized and distributed nature, the operation of the Bismuth Network, Bismuth Bridge and Bismuth ETH Oracle does not fall under the legislation of the USA nor the EEC.
End users local legislation may apply. It's up to the user to check whether usage of the service(s) are compliant to their local legislation.

Fees

A fixed fees of $BIS {{ bismuth['extra']['bis_fixed_fees'] }} applies to every swap. wBIS are ERC-20, proxy minted on ETH chain by the end user.
